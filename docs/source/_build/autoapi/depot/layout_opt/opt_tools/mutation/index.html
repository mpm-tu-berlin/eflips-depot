depot.layout_opt.opt_tools.mutation.mutgaussian_area_count_crop(depot, mu, sigma)¶

Random gaussian mutation of area count. If less than before, the area list is cropped on the right. If higher than before, new areas with random init values are appended to the right.

depot.layout_opt.opt_tools.mutation.mutgaussian_area_count_rnd(depot, mu, sigma)¶

Random gaussian mutation of area count. If less than before, random areas are removed. If higher than before, new areas with random capacities are appended to the right.

depot.layout_opt.opt_tools.mutation.mutuniform_area_count_crop(depot, a, b)¶

Random uniform mutation of area count. If less than before, the area list is cropped on the right. If higher than before, new areas with random init values are appended to the right.

depot.layout_opt.opt_tools.mutation.mutuniform_area_count_rnd(depot, a, b)¶

Random uniform mutation of area count. If less than before, the area list is cropped on the right. If higher than before, new areas with random init values are appended to the right.
